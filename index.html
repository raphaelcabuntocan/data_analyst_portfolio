World Energy Economy

PostgreSQL together with pgAdmin 4 was used to perform exploratory data analysis and data cleaning on a 22,000-row by 130-column dataset, resulting in 82 percent optimization of data size.

As a mechanical engineer, energy is one of the things that we specialize in. Energy is, literally, needed to get things done. It is crucial for the development and prosperity of nations. Here, data analysis and aggregation were performed producing 80 table columns which were used to answer 24 analytical questions centered on global electrical energy supply, sources and demand. These data will be used for further correlational and predictive analyses in Python of energy mixes, supply and demand changes, and country population.
